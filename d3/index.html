Process engineer. Brand strategist. Systems architect.

That failure led me here. Fifteen years of helping technology companies close the gap between what they've built and what the world is willing to believe.

I've watched a rebrand nearly destroy a client's trust because we moved faster than their product team. I learned that a bridge built too early is just a cliff. That lesson lives in everything I do now.

Today, as Creative Intelligence Officer at RedPeak — part of the kyu Collective — I lead strategic engagements for the world's largest semiconductor foundry, global semiconductor giants, edge AI storage leaders, industrial IoT pioneers, and precision test & measurement companies who need their story to match their substance.
